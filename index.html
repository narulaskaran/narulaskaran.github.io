4K for Cancer

In the summer of 2018, I spent two months running across the country for young adults with cancer. The Ulman Foundation is a nonprofit based in Baltimore, MD that focuses on providing a support network to young people affected by cancer.

For 49 days, I ran 60+ mile weeks with a team of 23 others as we traveled from San Francisco to Boston. Stopping at hospitals, Ronald McDonald Houses, and Hope Lodges along the way, I got to actually meet the kinds of people I was raising money for and perform service.
